Coding!!!

Problem solving is my greatest passion!

In my second year of PhD, I encountered a problem. In my experiment, the sample underwent a complex combination of multiple phenomena like diffusion, oxidation and mechanical deformation, that I could not solve with equations. Using the little Matlab coding I learnt for my Kinetics class, I spent a few months teaching myself more Matlab and ended up designing a specimen evolution model for my PhD with my limited knowledge! The first time I solved Hanoi tower, my mind was blown away by the power of recursion and the simplicity of getting a complicated task done through the computer. I find that the possibilities and capabilities that come with coding are limitless. It is our own limitation in applying these methods and tools, that are boundaries we can break through.
