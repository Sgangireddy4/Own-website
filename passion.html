Coding!!!

Problem solving is my greatest passion!

In my second year of PhD, my sample was undergoing a complex combination of multiple phenomena (diffusion, oxidation and mechanical deformation) during my experiment. I could not solve the state with equations. Using the little Matlab coding I knew I designed a specimen evolution model. That was an eye-opening experience into power of coding. I knew so little just loops etc, but I could apply it in so many ways!
Then the first time I solved Hanoi tower, my mind was blown away by the power of recursion and the simplicity of the algorithm! I feel the possibilities and capabilities that come with coding are limitless.

It is our own limitations in applying these methods and tools, which are boundaries we have to break through!

That is the perfect kind of challenge there is.
